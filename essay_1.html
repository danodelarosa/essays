Scott Berkun - Creative Thinking Hacks:
Is there a hack for creativity? well, there is not a hack at all, but in this video Scott gives you some tricks for having more creative moments

  • Combination: If you can not get a new idea, try to make a combination of two different ideas

  • Inhibition: try, try, try and try, every new mistake is guiding you to a new idea with fewer errors

  • Environment: there are a lot of people sharing his own way of being creative as "The true way of being creative" but this is no a fact, not everyone has the same feeling about creativity, find your own environment where you are more creative, maybe at night, in the morning, taking a shower or walking out with your pets, I dont know, find that way and use it.

  • Persistence: being creative is a long way, do no try to be the fastest guy of creativity because you can burnout yourself, be persistence, try a bit every day and never give up

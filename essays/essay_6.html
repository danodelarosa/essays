( November 16 - November 22 )

During this second week of the second phase I received a talk about "The art of feedback" and this was very interesting and helpful for me because I did not feel comfortable receiving feedback and during all these past phases I received a lot of comments, I had learned some things in the road, but in this talk a learned some tips to face these feelings and not only how to receive, even how to give a useful feedback

Receiving feedback:

  • Do not wait until the bomb explodes, ask for feedback regularly
  • Do not make it personal
  • Make sure the feedback, expectations and consequences are clear
  • Do not respond just by justifying your self, instead respond with a reflection

Giving feedback:

  • Do not delay and make things worse
  • Be empathic
  • be clear about feedback, expectations and consequences
  • Do not be nice, be helpful
  • Make the person fell safe

Feedback is about the actions, not about the persons, because actions can be changed

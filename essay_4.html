Chaos Engineering: the history, principles, and practice
Brief history of Chaos engineering

  • Chaos monkey
  • Simian Army
  • FIT (Failure Injection Testing)
  • Gremlin
  • Chaos conf
  • AWS - FIS (Failure Injection Simulation)
  • State of chaos engineering report

How it works?

  1. Plan an experiment
  2. Contain the blast radius of testing
  3. Scale or Fix

Types of Chaos Engineering Experiments:

  • Known Knowns - Things you are aware of and understand
  • Known Unknowns - Things you are aware of but don’t fully understand
  • Unknown Knowns - Things you understand but are not aware of
  • Unknown Unknowns - Things you are neither aware of nor fully

How to plan your first chaos experiment?

  1. Create a hypthesis
  2. Measure the impact
  3. Have a rollback plan
  4. Go fix it
